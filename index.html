Who We Are?

Balaji Auto Tech Pvt Ltd manufactures CNC machines, founded in 2002 by Mr. G.P. Satish. We offer CNC Plasma and Oxy-Fuel Cutting Machines, along with machine tool retrofits. As a leading exporter and supplier, we excel in precision engineering.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in every aspect of our machines, from design to production. Utilizing advanced technology and software, we deliver reliable, high-performance CNC machines tailored to our customers' needs. Complying with international quality standards, we rigorously test our products at all stages. Driven by a skilled team, we continuously refine our offerings to ensure maximum accuracy, efficiency, and productivity.

FAQ's?

What industries utilize CNC machining?

Industries such as aerospace, automotive, defence, electronics, shipbuilding, construction, fabrication and manufacturing frequently use CNC machining for precision parts production.

What CNC services do you offer?

We offer a range of services, including retrofitment, reconditioning, refurbishment and upgradation.

How can I request a quote?

You can request a quote by contacting us through our website or directly via email/phone with project specifications/requirements.

What are advantages of CNC machines?

CNC machines are automated and controlled by computer programs providing accurate cuts and shapes, ensuring high and consistent quality. CNC machines can significantly reduce production time, enhancing overall productivity.
